Who We Are

A True Family Business

Richardson and Sons Contractors got its humbling beginnings 43 years ago when Mabuto Richardson started working under a skilled carpenter in Queens, New York at the young age of 11. After falling in love with the profession, Mabuto further perfected his craft at Thomas Edison Trade School in New York City.

Having a genuine love for working with wood and being able to bring a customer’s vision to life, Mabuto knew he found his true calling. Eager to share his passion, Mabuto was then able to teach his son Devonte’ Smith the art of the craft. Thus Richardson and Sons Contracting was born.

A true family business that has a passion for helping the community, they believe the customer always comes first. Richardson and Sons value the customer’s time, insight, and investment being made. They are committed to providing high quality services to meet the wants and needs of those they serve.

In meeting the needs of customers, Richardson and Sons recognize that due to the constantly changing economy, services offered are affordable and conducive to the client. Specializing in decks, fences, interior/exterior trim, painting, remodeling, and much more — the duo can truly do it all.
